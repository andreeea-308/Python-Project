## Title: Watchmen

A Graphic Novel book by Alan Moore & Dave Gibbons.

Target Audience: Adult

Main Themes: superheroes, moral complexity, power, vigilantes, nuclear war, conspiracy

Summary:
A murdered hero draws former vigilantes into a conspiracy in an alternate 1985 America. Their investigation spirals into a moral dilemma about ends and means as nuclear war looms. The book dismantles the superhero myth and asks who holds power accountable.

Key Elements:
- Genre: Graphic Novel
- Themes: superheroes, moral complexity, power, vigilantes, nuclear war, conspiracy
- Perfect for readers who enjoy: Graphic Novel

Why read this book:
This book offers a compelling story that explores superheroes, moral complexity, power. 
A murdered hero draws former vigilantes into a conspiracy in an alternate 1985 America. Readers will find themselves engaged with the narrative and its deeper meanings.
