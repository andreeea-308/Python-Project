## Title: The Lean Startup

A Business / Entrepreneurship book by Eric Ries.

Target Audience: Adult

Main Themes: entrepreneurship, startup, business strategy, innovation, product development

Summary:
Startups win by learning faster than uncertainty can kill them. Build a Minimum Viable Product, measure real user behavior, then learn whether to pivot or persevere. Replace vanity metrics with 'innovation accounting' so evidence, not ego, guides the roadmap.

- Genre: Business / Entrepreneurship
- Themes: entrepreneurship, startup, business strategy, innovation, product development
