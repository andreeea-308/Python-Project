## Title: The Miracle of Mindfulness

A Mindfulness / Spirituality book by Thich Nhat Hanh.

Target Audience: Adult

Main Themes: mindfulness, meditation, spirituality, presence, buddhism, peace

Summary:
Nhat Hanh offers simple practices for bringing full attention to breathing, chores, and work. Mindfulness becomes a practical skill for calm, clarity, and compassion. Short exercises show how presence can change how you respond to stress.

Key Elements:
- Genre: Mindfulness / Spirituality
- Themes: mindfulness, meditation, spirituality, presence, buddhism, peace
- Perfect for readers who enjoy: Mindfulness / Spirituality
