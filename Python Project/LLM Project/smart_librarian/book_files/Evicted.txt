## Title: Evicted

A Sociology / Urban Studies book by Matthew Desmond.

Target Audience: Adult

Main Themes: poverty, housing, eviction, social inequality, urban studies, policy

Summary:
Through immersive reporting in Milwaukee, Desmond maps how eviction drives poverty. Families and landlords are caught in a system where high rent, instability, and debt feed each other. The result is a data-rich case for policy and legal reforms to keep people housed.
