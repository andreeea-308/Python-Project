## Title: Atomic Habits

A Self-Help book by James Clear.

Target Audience: Adult

Main Themes: habits, productivity, self-improvement, behavior change, personal development

Summary:
Small, consistent improvements compound into outsized results. Clear explains the cue–craving–response–reward loop and how to redesign your environment. The core move is identity-based change—become the kind of person who does the habit, then let behavior follow.

Key Elements:
- Genre: Self-Help
- Themes: habits, productivity, self-improvement, behavior change, personal development
- Perfect for readers who enjoy: Self-Help

Why read this book:
This book offers a compelling story that explores habits, productivity, self-improvement. 
Small, consistent improvements compound into outsized results. Readers will find themselves engaged with the narrative and its deeper meanings.
