## Title: Thinking, Fast and Slow

A Cognitive Psychology book by Daniel Kahneman.

Target Audience: Adult

Main Themes: psychology, decision making, cognitive bias, behavioral economics, thinking

Summary:
Kahneman maps two modes of thought: fast, intuitive System 1 and slow, analytical System 2. He shows how biases—loss aversion, anchoring, availability—distort judgment. The takeaway: we can't delete bias, but we can design decisions to blunt it.

- Genre: Cognitive Psychology
- Themes: psychology, decision making, cognitive bias, behavioral economics, thinking
